ABOUT

Welcome to my website! My name is Alexander Huxel, and I am an enthusiastic frontend developer with a passion for creative solutions and innovative web development.

My professional journey started with an apprenticeship as a specialist for warehouse logistics, which I successfully completed in 2014. I worked in this field until August 2021, most recently as the department manager of the logistics department in a mechanical engineering company.

However, I discovered my true passion in frontend development. Starting from November 2020, I dedicated all of my free time alongside my job to systematically train myself as a frontend developer through a career change. I continuously developed my skills, which I had already acquired and honed over the years, and demonstrated my knowledge in challenging projects.

With extensive experience in JavaScript, Angular, and CSS preprocessors like Sass/Scss, I aim to bring your ideas to life. I take pride in my comprehensive knowledge and experience in PHP and WordPress.

My goal is to achieve high-quality results. I work with focus, structure, discipline, and always maintain a professional approach.

In my current position as a frontend developer at an advertising agency in Karlsruhe, I have been able to collaborate with my team in developing demanding web projects and successfully applying agile methods such as Scrum.

I look forward to getting to know your visions and creating tailored solutions that exceed your expectations.
